About Acronavis

Built to spark curiosity and show what innovation can feel like.

From product to possibility

Dozarc Innovation creates a new category of intelligent robots. What looks like a toy is designed as an entry point into real engineering, modular construction and immersive storytelling. Every piece is built to move beyond play and open the door to larger technological possibilities.

Real technology

Our products are autonomus self propulsion systems designed to feel simple and intuitive. Built from 111 components and connected through a mobile app, they bring together hardware, software and interaction in a way that can grow far beyond the initial experience.

Made to stand out

Co-designed with psychologists and educators, every Dozarc product balances hands-on building, experimentation and genuine learning. Dozarc introduces a fully developed science fiction universe, creating a world users can explore, expand and grow with over time.
